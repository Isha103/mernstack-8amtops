IT Hub

An "IT Hub" refers to a geographical location that serves as a central point for information technology development, innovation, and business activity, typically characterized by a high concentration of tech companies, startups, research institutions, and skilled IT professionals, fostering collaboration and driving technological advancements within the area. .
